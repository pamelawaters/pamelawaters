JungL App

JungL is a dating app based on Carl Jung's personality theories. JungL was founded to help humans connect to the core of nature; and at the core of human nature we believe is human connection. Humans should aim to connect to each other from the core and not only superficially.

I designed and produced all creative materials for the company a few years back.
